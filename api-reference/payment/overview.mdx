The Payments API allows your platform to initiate money movement **from one of your registered users to another user or payee**.  
Payments can be funded through a user’s **wallet**, **card**, **bank (ACH)**, or **Paywint bank account**, and each method follows its own processing timeline.

This section covers all APIs related to:
- Sending money to a user or payee
- Executing transfers using wallet, card, ACH, or Paywint Bank
- Calculating processing and delivery fees
- Mailing physical checks
- Retrieving sent or received payment history
- Fetching payment details by ID

---

## How Payments Work

Before initiating a payment, your platform must:
1. **Create the user** in Paywint  
2. Add at least one **funding method** to the user (wallet, card, bank, or Paywint bank account)  
3. **Create a payee** for the user (for card, bank, or check payments)  

Once these steps are complete, the business can initiate payments using the `/create-payment` endpoint.

---

## Supported Payment Methods

### **Wallet (Instant)**
- Primary and fastest method  
- Amount is deducted from the payer’s Paywint wallet and instantly delivered to the payee  
- No bank delays and low processing overhead  

### **Card (1–2 business days)**
- Moves money using the payer’s linked debit card  
- Processing may take up to two business days  
- Typically used when users do not maintain a wallet balance  

### **Bank ACH (3–4 business days)**
- Uses the payer’s linked bank account  
- Slowest but commonly used for higher-value payments  
- Standard ACH processing timelines apply  

### **Paywint Bank Account**
- For users with a Paywint-issued virtual account  
- Seamless internal transfer flow  
- Fast and fully traceable

## Payment Flows Covered in This Section

### **Create Payment**
Initiates a payment between two users or a user and their payee.  
The API:
- Validates the payer, payee, wallet, and payment method  
- Calculates processing and delivery fees  
- Executes the correct flow depending on payment method  
- Generates transaction notifications  
- Triggers relevant webhooks  

### **List Payments**
Retrieve:
- All platform payments  
- All payments initiated by a specific user  
- All payments received by a specific user  

Includes pagination support.

### **Get Payment**
Fetch a payment’s complete details using its unique ID.

### **Mail Check Payments**
Send physical checks via USPS First Class, Certified, Overnight, and Two-Day delivery.  
Supports PDF attachments (up to 7MB, 10 pages).

## When to Use the Payments API

Use the Payments API when your platform needs to:
- Allow users to send money to each other
- Pay contractors, service providers, or merchants
- Support card, bank, wallet, or Paywint bank transfers
- Generate physical checks for offline payees
- Retrieve or audit historical outgoing/incoming payments

For collecting payments **from** customers (e-commerce/checkout), see **Accept Payments**.
