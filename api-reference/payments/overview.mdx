The Payments API enables secure money movement between users and payees.  
Payments can be funded through a user’s **wallet**, **linked card**, **bank (ACH)**, or **Paywint Bank account**, and delivered instantly or through scheduled settlement timelines.

This section covers how to create payments, mail checks, and retrieve payment records.

---

## How Payments Work

Before sending a payment, ensure that:

1. **The payer is a registered user**
2. **The recipient exists as either:**
   - Another registered user, or  
   - A payee with a saved card, bank, or mailing address

Once this setup is complete, payments can be created using the  
**[Create Payment](/api-reference/payments/create)** API.

---

## Supported Payment Methods

### **Wallet (Instant Delivery)**
- Fastest payment method  
- Funds are deducted from the payer’s wallet  
- Delivered instantly  

---

### **Card (1–2 business days)**
- Pulls funds from the payer’s linked card
- Typical settlement: 1–2 business days  

---

### **Bank ACH (3–4 business days)**
- Debits the payer’s linked bank account  
- Common for high-value transfers  
- ACH settlement timeline applies  

---

### **Paywint Bank Account**
- Uses the user’s Paywint-issued virtual account  
- Fast and fully traceable  

---

### **Physical Check (Mail Delivery)**
- Creates and mails a physical check to a payee  
- Supports USPS First Class, Certified, Two-Day, and Overnight  
- Optional PDF attachments (up to 7MB, 10 pages)  

---

## Available Payment APIs

### **Create Payment**
Initiates a payment from one user to another user or payee.

This API automatically:
- Validates payer, payee, and payment method  
- Calculates applicable processing and delivery fees  
- Executes the correct payment flow (wallet, card, ACH, Paywint Bank)  
- Sends payment lifecycle notifications  
- Returns the complete payment object  

 **[Create Payment](/api-reference/payments/create)**

---

### **Mail Check**
Creates and mails a physical check to a payee’s address.  
Includes:

- Attachment validation  
- Fee calculation based on mail type  
- Wallet deduction  
- Check generation and mailing  

 **[Mail Check](/api-reference/payments/mail-check)**

---

### **Get Payment**
Fetch complete details for a specific payment using its ID.  
Includes amounts, fees, payment method, status, timestamps, and destination information.

 **[Get Payment](/api-reference/payments/get)**

---

### **List Payments**
Retrieve all payments, with filtering and pagination.

 **[List Payments](/api-reference/payments/list)**

---

### **List User Payments**
Retrieve all payments initiated by a specific user.

 **[List User Payments](/api-reference/payments/list-sent)**

---

### **List User Received Payments**
Retrieve all payments received by a specific user.

 **[List Received Payments](/api-reference/payments/list-user-received-payments)**
